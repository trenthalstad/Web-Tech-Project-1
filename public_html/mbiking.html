Mountain Biking in Montana

The Views


Nothing is quite as satisfying as the view off the top of a mountain you just conquered. Peaks, valleys, natural waterfalls, huckleberries, moose...the list goes on. There are so many different wonders you can see as you pedal your way up a Montana mountain. We'll just say the trudge up won't be the only thing that will take your breath away.

Featured Trail of the Month

Jewel Basin Trail

Only a half hour from downtown Kalispell but miles away from civilization. A day hiker’s paradise, the Jewel’s 25 lakes stud its 35-mile, well-marked trail system. Mount Aeneas, its highest point, can be tackled in a day, 8 miles round-trip.
